Activity 1: Base/Superstructure and Hegemony

Tomat0's Political Perspective Assessment

This first activity will have you sorting the three hegemonies: cultural hegemony, material hegemony, and civic hegemony into a base/superstructure format. In other words, which of the following do you think is the root cause (base) of the others (superstructure)?

  • Hegemony: The dominant order which justifies the status quo/hierarchy
    • Cultural Hegemony: The process by which the ideas and values of a society justify the status quo (ex; religion/morality, mainstream media, ideology, political culture)
    • Civic Hegemony: The process by which the coercive force of a person or group reinforces the status quo (ex; Social Darwinism, existence of government, imperialism)
    • Material Hegemony: The process by which distribution of resources justifies the status quo (ex; means of production, wealth, private property, or economy)
  • Base: The aspect(s) of society responsible for shaping the rest of society, the root cause of political issues.(ex; sin nature, capitalism, hierarchy)
  • Superstructure: All other aspects of society not included in the base, serves the purpose of maintaining the base.
